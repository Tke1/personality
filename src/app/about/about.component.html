Our Story

We started Wag Match in 2017 to help returned shelter dogs in finding their mates. During St.Louis Puria Hackerton, we realized that there is a high return rate of adopted dogs from animal shelters in St. Louis area. To us, this is a heartbreaking news. With this application, we match prospects with shelter dogs using scentific compatibility assessements to reduce age and breed bias.
